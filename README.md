R&R Stranded Ships Operations

R&R Ops is an organization which's primary function is to recover sensitive tech and rescue personnel in difficult and, sometimes, impossible situations. 

They are contractors for the Republic, Deep Security, Syndicate and even the Free Worlds(after they separate themselves from the Republic).

To start your path as a freelancer extractor for R&R Ops, simply head to Mars and check the spaceport. After a very fast intro mission, the jobs will show up on your job board always following the same flow of steps:
1. Accept the job and head to the specified system
2. Board the disable objective ship to perform your job
3. Now it's up to you. Will you quickly land on the planet or will you fight the pirates that also want the tech/crew you just saved?

There are currently 3 tiers of jobs.
1. The "easy" tier is automatically unlocked after the intro mission
2. The "mid" tier has its unlock mission available on Ada in Aldebaran system after either you complete 20 jobs for R&R Ops and have combat rank 6 OR after you complete 40 jobs
3. the "hard" tier unlock mission will be instantly offered to the player if the mid tier was unlocked AND either the player completed 50 jobs and has combat rank 9 OR completed 100 jobs.

The Pirate ambushes scale with the job tier!

The first release is completely functional but I'll be adding more missions and optimizing R&R Ops as soon as I learn more about content creation.
